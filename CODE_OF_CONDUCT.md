# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

We are committed to a respectful, inclusive, and constructive community.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and professional.
- Focus on technical substance and collaborative problem-solving.
- Offer and receive feedback in good faith.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, discrimination, or personal attacks.
- Doxxing or disclosure of private/personal information.
- Disruptive conduct that prevents productive collaboration.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers are responsible for enforcement and may remove or restrict participation for violations.

Report concerns to: `maintainer@example.org`.
